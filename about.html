THIS IS HACKERS UNDER 20

Our Mission

HACKERS UNDER 20 was established for the purpose of getting more high school students interested in hackathons. As a network of hackathons around the United States, We hopes to facilitate the interactions between high school hackathons and grow attendance numbers. HACKERS UNDER 20 also offers a variety of resources to help students launch high school hackathons. Together in this endeavour, we hope to grow the number of “hackers” in the United States and ultimately contribute toward the Computer Science collective efforts.

The mission of HACKERS UNDER 20 is to establish a network of hackathons, workshops, and other coding resources to help progress computer science education across the globe. Hackathons are already enough of a challenge to the organizers as they have been planning for months in advance. HACKERS UNDER 20 aims to support those organizers with some of their most difficult tasks including logistical planning, obtaining sponsorships, and getting people hacking!

As hackers ourselves, we want to see more adolescents having basic code literacy and being introduced to the fascinating world of computer science. We hope that by supporting people around the world to hold epic hackathons and workshops, we can spread how awesome computer science to students of all ages.
