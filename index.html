About Us

HACKERS UNDER 20 was established for the purpose of getting more high school students interested in hackathons. As a network of hackathons around the United States, we hopes to facilitate the interactions between high school hackathons and grow attendance numbers. HACKERS UNDER 20 also offers a variety of resources to help students launch high school hackathons. Together in this endeavour, we hope to grow the number of “hackers” in the United States and ultimately contribute toward the Computer Science collective efforts.
